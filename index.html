Lion

The Lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, broad-chested body; short, rounded head; round ears; and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

Tiger

The Tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera. It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside.

Cheetah

The Cheetah is a large cat native of africa and central iran. it is the fastestland animal, capable of running at 80 to 128 km/h (50 to 80 mph).
